This trend could reduce the demand for the most powerful and expensive NVIDIA GPUs, enabling the use of less potent or alternative hardware for many AI applications.

While major tech giants continue to develop their own custom AI accelerators, posing a long-term threat to NVIDIA's general-purpose GPU demand, the immediate impact on smaller AI startups is often seen through the choices of cloud providers. However, the increasing viability and strategic adoption of non-NVIDIA solutions by both large and emerging AI players suggest a more fragmented and competitive landscape is forming.
