## Key Findings

- **CoWoS Utilization**: TSMC's advanced packaging running at only 60% capacity, suggesting potential demand softness
- **China Market**: Complex dynamics with H20 sales resumption but 15% revenue sharing requirement
- **Blackwell Ramp**: Fastest product ramp in NVIDIA history with improved yields
- **Competition**: Growing adoption of alternatives for inference workloads, though NVIDIA maintains training dominance
